需要预先安装好jdk8+

已经下载好scala包放置于software目录下

```
# tar -zxvf scala-2.13.1.tgz -C ~/app/
# vi ~/.bash_profile
export SCALA_HOME=/root/app/scala-2.13.1
export PATH=$SCALA_HOME/bin:$PATH
# source ~/.bash_profile
```



非常的简单哟
